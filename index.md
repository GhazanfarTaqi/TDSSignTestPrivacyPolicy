# Privacy Policy for TDS Quiz App

**Last Updated:** March 11, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to TDS Quiz App ("we," "us," "our," or "Company"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Quiz Responses:** Your answers to quiz questions are stored locally on your device only. We do not transmit quiz data to our servers.
- **Language Preference:** Your selected language (English/Urdu) is stored locally on your device.

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information:** Device model, OS version, device ID (for ad personalization)
- **Usage Analytics:** Quiz completion status, time spent in the app
- **IP Address:** Your approximate location based on IP address
- **Crash Reports:** Error logs to help us improve the app

### 2.3 Information from Third-Party Services
We use **Google Admob** to display advertisements. Google collects:
- Device identifiers (Android Advertising ID)
- Approximate location
- Ad interaction data
- IP address

**See Google's Privacy Policy:** https://policies.google.com/privacy

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Display personalized and non-personalized ads
- Improve app performance and user experience
- Fix bugs and technical issues
- Analyze app usage patterns
- Send important app updates and notifications

## 4. Advertising

This app uses **Google AdMob** to serve advertisements. AdMob may:
- Display banner ads during your quiz
- Show interstitial (full-screen) ads after quiz completion
- Use your device information to deliver relevant ads
- Track ad performance and user engagement

You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings:
- **Android:** Settings → Google → Manage your Google Account → Data & Privacy → Ad Settings

## 5. Data Storage and Retention

- **Quiz Data:** Stored locally on your device. Not transmitted to our servers.
- **Ad Data:** Retained by Google according to their retention policies (typically 13 months)
- **Crash Reports:** Retained for up to 90 days for troubleshooting

## 6. Data Sharing

We do **NOT** sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:
- **Google Admob:** For ad delivery and analytics
- **Crash Reporting Tools:** To diagnose technical issues
- **Legal Compliance:** If required by law

## 7. Children's Privacy

TDS Quiz App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to remove such information and terminate the child's account.

## 8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 9. Your Rights and Choices

### 9.1 Access and Control
You can:
- Clear app data from your device settings
- Remove the app entirely
- Opt out of personalized ads in your device settings
- Reset your advertising ID

### 9.2 CCPA Rights (California Residents)
If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

- Delete personal information collected

### 9.3 GDPR Rights (EU Residents)
If you are an EU resident, you have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Request deletion ("right to be forgotten")
- Restrict processing
- Data portability
